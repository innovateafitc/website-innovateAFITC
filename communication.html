Communication

We encourage you to join our SLACK channels to communicate with event staff, subject matter experts and one another. In addition, the below platforms are great FREE resources to have team virtual meetings to collaborate during the competition.

Zoom

Zoom is a cloud-based video communications app that allows you to set up virtual video and audio conferencing, webinars, live chats, screen-sharing, and other collaborative capabilities.

Google Meet

Google Meet (formerly Hangouts Meet) is a video-communication service developed by Google.

Jitsi

Jitsi is a collection of free and open-source multiplatform voice, videoconferencing and instant messaging applications for the web platform, Windows, Linux, macOS, iOS and Android.
